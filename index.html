Computer Build

Spring 2010

Sophomore year of highschool I decided I wanted to try to build a computer. The ability to pick and choose components seemed very appealing to me. Having a very basic knowledge of what components might be necessary, I took this opportunity to learn what lived inside the case, which until then I hadn't given a second thought.

Wanting to challenge myself with the computer build I decided to mod the case which would house my new computer. I cut out a portion of the side panel framing the components with a plexiglass window, as well as cutting out vent holes in order to increase airflow. Finally I decided to paint the computer as whole since I thought it would be more appealing than leaving the case as is.

This long term project taught me a lot about planning ahead and having patience since I had to make sure my project was complete before I could actually set up my new rig. It also served as an opportunity to enter a community I hadn't been a part of before by actively participating in a forum which allowed me to learn more about modding, as well as being helpful in deciding the components I'd get in order to make my new computer.
